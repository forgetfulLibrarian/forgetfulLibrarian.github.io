Forgetful Librarian

Love My party people

Jimmy at the door askin “Can I come in?” “Can I come in Sue can I come in?” Not yet Jimmy cuz I know where ya been. I love you but I know your sins. Jimmy at the door askin “Why not now?” “Why not now Sue Why not now?” You could be forgiven but I don’t know how. I love you but I will not bow. Jimmy at the door saying “You’re no fun. “You’re no fun Sue You’re no fun” You could come in if you didn’t have a gun. I love you but I think we’re done.
